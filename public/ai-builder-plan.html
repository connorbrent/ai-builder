What I'm Building
Rank 1  ·  Highest Impact
Voicemails Route to the Right CM — Without Connor Touching Them
Quick Win 5–8 hrs/week Not Started
A missed call leaves a voicemail. That voicemail is transcribed and routed directly to the right community manager — no Connor in the loop.
What's Happening Today
Connor forwarded 5 Zoom voicemails in a single hour today. They arrive from the main line (Ext. 500) and land in his inbox as attachments. He reads, identifies the community, and forwards to the right person — every time.
What the Automated Version Looks Like
Trigger: Zoom voicemail email arrives. Claude reads the transcription, identifies caller + community from number or name, routes directly to the correct CM with a summary. Unknown callers go to the support queue. Zero Connor involvement. Buildable in 2–3 hours.
Rank 2
New Client Onboarding Runs Without Connor as a Checkpoint
Medium Lift 4–6 hrs / onboarding Not Started
When a new community signs, the questionnaire, transition sheet, and welcome letter are generated and sent — automatically. Connor never touches a document.
What's Happening Today
Summit Court: Questionnaire iterated twice in two days, Transition Sheet twice in one day, Welcome Letter revised — 5 doc touches for one client. Calendar shows "Vantaca/Onboarding Escalations" as a recurring personal task. Lakewood Shores is incoming now.
What the Automated Version Looks Like
Trigger: new community added in Vantaca or HomeWiseDocs. Claude pulls community data, populates master templates (questionnaire, transition sheet, welcome letter). Community manager gets a draft for one-click review and send. Connor never sees it.
Rank 3
Homeowner Inquiries Get Drafted Responses — Team Just Approves
Medium Lift 5–7 hrs/week Not Started
Every repetitive homeowner email has a drafted response waiting in the queue — staff reviews and hits send.
What's Happening Today
Dues complaints, account issues, insurance questions, escrow requests — all landing in Connor's inbox first, then forwarded to the right person. Sent Items has 3,952 emails all-time. Each requires reading, categorizing, and routing before anyone can respond.
What the Automated Version Looks Like
Trigger: inbound email to support inbox. Claude categorizes (dues / violation / maintenance / escrow), pulls Vantaca account data, drafts a response in the Lifetime voice. Team sees: Category · Draft · Approve or Edit. Connor removed from the loop entirely.
Rank 4
Homeowner Calls Get Answered by AI on the First Ring
Big Build 8–12 hrs/week Not Started
CA Sales completes 80%+ of calls because an AI voice agent handles tier-1 questions before the call reaches a human or voicemail.
What's Happening Today
CA Sales: 5 of 37 calls completed (13.5%). CA Vendors: 0%. Every missed call becomes a voicemail Connor routes manually. One missed intake at 23821 Hillhurst became a 5-day gap, an apology to an escrow agent, and a CEO escalation.
What the Automated Version Looks Like
Trigger: inbound call to any queue. AI voice agent answers, pulls homeowner record from Vantaca by phone number, handles FAQs (dues balance, violation status, meeting dates, payment portal). Complex issues get a live transfer with a context summary ready.
Rank 5  ·  Quick Win
Session Learning Converts to Employee SOPs Within 24 Hours
Quick Win 2–3 hrs/week Not Started
Every Leland AI Builder session produces a Word-format employee SOP in SharePoint by the next morning — no writing time from Connor.
What's Happening Today
Connor is in L1 + L2 simultaneously through May 8. Knowledge stays in his head after each session. The team (Pam, Brian, Kathy, Rebecca) can't act on it until Connor writes it up — which may not happen before paternity leave.
What the Automated Version Looks Like
Trigger: upload session notes or recording transcript after each session. Claude converts to step-by-step employee SOP in M365 Word format, plain language. Saves to SharePoint automatically. "Automate Communication in Your Voice" becomes a usable guide the next day.
Rank 6  ·  Quick Win
Weekly Phone + Email Ops Summary Lands in Inbox Automatically
Quick Win 1 hr/week Not Started
Every Monday, call queue completion rates, voicemail volume, and top email patterns are waiting in Connor's inbox — no manual report pulling.
What's Happening Today
Call queue data manually downloaded as a CSV. No automatic alerting means the CA Sales 13.5% completion rate could persist for weeks undetected. Community email volumes visible only by manually checking folder counts in Outlook.
What the Automated Version Looks Like
Trigger: Monday 8am cron. Scheduled Claude agent pulls Zoom API for call queue metrics, checks email volumes by community folder, flags anything below threshold, emails a formatted summary. Setup time: under 2 hours using Claude Code + the schedule skill.
